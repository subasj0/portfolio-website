Privacy Policy for Suchak

Effective date: April 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Suchak ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use our WhatsApp-based assistant and related services (the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

  • Messages and prompts: Questions and content you send to the Suchak WhatsApp assistant.
  • Usage data: Technical logs such as timestamps, approximate region, device type, and interaction metadata needed to operate and improve the Service.
  • Contact identifier: Your WhatsApp phone number and profile information made available through WhatsApp/Meta.
  • Public market data: We use publicly available data sources, including Nepal Stock Exchange (NEPSE) related data, to answer your questions.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide responses to your questions and fulfill Service functionality.
  • Maintain security, prevent abuse, and troubleshoot issues.
  • Improve response quality, reliability, and user experience.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data only as needed with:

  • Service providers that help us host, secure, and operate the Service.
  • Platform partners (such as Meta/WhatsApp) to deliver messaging features.
  • Authorities when required by law, court order, or to protect rights and safety.

4. Data Retention

We keep personal data only for as long as necessary to provide the Service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. We may retain de-identified or aggregated data for analytics and performance improvement.

5. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Financial Information Disclaimer

Suchak may provide information related to stocks, including NEPSE data, for informational purposes only. It is not financial, investment, legal, or tax advice.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of your personal data. You can submit requests using the contact details below.

8. Children

The Service is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own, where data protection laws may differ.
